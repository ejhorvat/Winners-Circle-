About the Film

The Emerson family, Sam, Michael and Lucy move to their grandfathers house in Nothern California after a recent devorce. Soon after their arrival Michael falls for a girl named star, who leads him into the hideout of the Lost Boys. David the 'leader' of the group convinces Michael to become 'one of us'. In the meantime Sam met two boys who claim to be fighters agaisnt a evil force, the frog brothers explain to Sam that there is something wrong with the place they live. Little do they know The Lost Boys are the evil they are talking about.. Vampires. Sam must save Michael from the Dangers of the Lost Boys, and himself..
